Privacy Policy

First off, we are thrilled that you took the time to utilize our services. You probably already understand what TaskTracker does, but for the sake of redundancy; TaskTracker allows you to maintain your daily personal or work activities in an organized manner. To maintain the quality of our service, there is potentially private information you share with us. This information can come in many forms, for example by contacting us through the app, we use that information to make our services better for you– this affords you more content and services. We want to be clear about how we’re using this information and the ways in which you can protect your privacy.

Our Privacy Policy explains: 

1) What information we collect and why we collect it. 

2) How we use that information.

Your privacy matters to us so whether you are new to TaskTracker or a longtime user, please do take the time to get to know our practices – and if you have any questions contact us at tasktracker2021@gmail.com or through the app.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from basic stuff like your location while using our app, to more sensitive information like your email and log information when you report a bug.

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you give us. Some optional features of TaskTracker require you to grant us recording audio permissions or location permissions.

Since these features are optional, denying these permissions will not affect the primary purpose of the app.

Information we get from your use of our services. We collect information about errors and crashes encountered while using TaskTracker. This allows us to improve the functionality and usability of the app. Some of the log information we collect is:

Device information

We collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, app version, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information). TaskTracker may associate this information with your user account.

Log information

When you use our services or view content provided by TaskTracker, we automatically collect and store certain information in server logs. This includes device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, your device date, time and locale.

Unique application numbers

Our services include a unique application number. This number and information about your installation (for example, the operating system type and application version number) may be sent to TaskTracker  when you install or uninstall that service or when that service periodically contacts our servers, such as for automatic updates.

Local storage

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device.

How we use the information we collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect TaskTracker and our users.

We may use the name you provide for your TaskTracker profile across all of the services we offer. If other users already have your email, or other information that identifies you, we may show them your publicly visible username. In future app updates, users may be able to send messages to you if they have your username or email used to register your TaskTracker account.

When you contact TaskTracker, we keep a record of your communication and device information to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements, or reply to you when you contact us through TaskTracker.

We use information collected from other technologies, to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. For example, by saving your language preferences, we’ll be able to have our services appear in the language you prefer. Or by saving your view settings, TaskTracker is able to launch with your preferred view settings already loaded.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

Accessing and updating your personal information

Whenever you use our services, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information. If that information is wrong, we strive to give you ways to update it quickly or to delete it – unless we have to keep that information for legitimate business or legal purposes.

We aim to maintain our services in a manner that protects information from accidental or malicious destruction. Because of this, after you delete information from our services, we may not immediately delete residual copies from our active servers and may not remove information from our backup systems.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of TaskTracker unless one of the following circumstances applies:

With your consent

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of TaskTracker when we have your consent to do so.

For external processing

We may provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

For legal reasons

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of TaskTracker if we have a goodfaith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.

enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations.

detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.

protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of TaskTracker, our users or the public as required or

permitted by law.

We may share non-personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners – like publishers, advertisers or connected sites. For example, we may share information publicly to show trends in the general use of our services.

If TaskTracker is involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes). We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.
